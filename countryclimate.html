Florida in Sub-Tropical

On average, Florida has the mildest winters in the Continental United States. Average lows range from 65 °F (18 °C) in Key West to near 41 °F (5 °C) degrees Fahrenheit at Tallahassee, while daytime highs range from 64 °F (18 °C) at Tallahassee to 77 °F (25 °C) at Miami.

Florida is one of the warmest region in the United States with an average daily high temperature of 28 degrees centigrade
